Colors
Robyn Comes with predefined set of colors. The colors used are personal choice of the creator and can be accessed by using the predefined classnames and also can be overrided by using classes.
Below is the list of different colors used by robyn along with their hex codes.
Primary #3D5AFE
Success #388E3C
Danger #FF3000
Warning #FFFF00
Black #000000
In addition to the above basic colors the color white: #fffff along with the two different shades #f1f1f1 and #f8f8f8. Along with this we have use three different shades of the primary color #00008b, #e3f2fd and #78909c

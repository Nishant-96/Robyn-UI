Badge
A Badge is a visual indicator for numeric values such as tallies and notifications pending. It is also used to show online and offline status of a person.
Badge on Icon
On icons badge are used to show the count of notifications or mails are pending. Here we use classes 'badge', 'icon-btn-pos' to position badge over the icons button with class 'icon-btn'.
                        
                            
    <button type="button" class="icon-btn">
        <span class="material-icons">notifications</span>
        <span class="badge icon-btn-pos badge-primary">10</span>
    </button>
    <button type="button" class="icon-btn">
        <span class="material-icons">mail</span>
        <span class="badge icon-btn-pos  badge-primary">10</span>
    </button>
Badge on Avatar
On avatar badges are used to show status on people's account whether they are online or offline. Here we use classes 'badge', 'badge-av' 'profile-av' and 'profile-av-pos' to position badge over the icons button with class 'badge-on' or 'badge-dnd' to show the status.
                        
                            
    <div class='badge-av'>
        <img src="../images/avatar.png" alt="avatar" class="profile-av"/>
        <span class="badge profile-av-pos badge-on"</span>
    </div>
                        
                        
    <div class='badge-av'>
        <img src="../images/avatar.png" alt="avatar" class="profile-av"/>
        <span class="badge profile-av-pos badge-dnd"</span>
    </div>

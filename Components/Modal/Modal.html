Modal
A Modal is a message box that is displayed on top of your screen. Modals put an overlay on the screen; therefore, they take visual precedence over all the other elements. Here we have used to buttons which are similar to our button components along with with the classes 'modal modal-title' as shown below.

Modal Title

Modal Body Goes Here

                                           

    <div class="modal">
        <h3 class="modal-title">Modal Title</h3>
        <p>Modal Body Goes Here</p>
        <div class="modal-btn">
                <button class="btn btn-primary">Save Changes</button>
                <button class="btn btn-outlined btn-outlined-danger">Close</button>
        </div>
    </div>
